De Dolibarr Wiki.

The easy to use OpenSource software for business. Be free to manage your small and medium company, freelance or foundation activity



This wiki contains all documentation (users, developers and translators documentation) and resources about Dolibarr ERP/CRM. Downloads are available on Dolibarr project web portal (go to http://www.dolibarr.org).
